About Bella Vista

A culinary journey of passion and excellence

Our Story

Since 1985, Bella Vista has been a cornerstone of fine dining, combining traditional culinary techniques with innovative flavors. Our commitment to excellence has earned us recognition as one of the city's premier dining destinations.

Led by Executive Chef Marco Antonelli, our kitchen team crafts each dish with meticulous attention to detail, using only the finest locally-sourced ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations.
